Now we will move on to the break down of each fuel type over the years 2001-2023
Sectors using fuel and its import/export

As you can see Tasmania stands out in terms of the renewable energy it uses, mostly hydro (water) and Northern territory stands out for its lack of renewable energy consumption and high non-renewable energy production. Refined products are imported the most due to a lack of making them and coal is exported the most due to the immense number of coal plants.
