Sectors using fuel and its import/export
Sectors using fuel and its import/export

As you can see, Tasmania stands out in terms of renewable energy usage, mostly hydro (water), while the Northern Territory has low renewable energy consumption and high non-renewable energy production. Refined products are imported the most due to a lack of domestic production, and coal is exported the most because of the extensive number of coal plants.
